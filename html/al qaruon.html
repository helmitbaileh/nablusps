Harat Al-Qaryoun

Harat Al-Qaryoun is one of the neighborhoods located in the old city of Nablus, situated to the east of Harat Al-Yasmeena. Most of its buildings date back to the Ottoman era. Among its prominent buildings are the Taqwan Palace, Al-Tinah Mosque, and the Sabana Taqwan. The neighborhood is centered around Tuta Square.
